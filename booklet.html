Heroes Booklet

Typography | Fall 2023

For this project, we were creating a booklet based on heroes which included people, organizations and ideas. We started with the sketches for the spreads and covers followed by their digital versions and variations.
Since the booklet isn't about superheroes, or just about certain types of people, we needed to keep it as neutral and diverse as we could. Later on, we put to the test our spreads to make sure everything is in place before sending it to print which was the last step.
