Icons Project

Graphic Design Principles II | Fall 2023

For this project, I chose the topic "What is Integration?". In the sketching process, I tried different ways of representing what I understood by integration using a variety of things like shapes, figures, people, etc; but, ultimately, decided to go for "social integration".
That is why the majority of my sketches have people and the planet earth on them. Following the next step, which was choosing two of my sketches and starting working on color palettes, I played with 3 different combinations (strong blue with yellow, browns/ oranges with light blue, and different tones of greens). However, after thinking about it and asking for comments, in the end I decided to go for this brown kind of orange with light blue because I think they help represent skin tones better in comparison to the other ones, which helps demonstrate my topic better and helps people (audience) understand it.
