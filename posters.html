John Maeda Posters

Graphic Design Principles II | Fall 2023

For this project we were working with a designer and I since I was working with John Maeda, I decided to use his book "Laws of Simplicity" as inspirationfor my posters. In this book, John Maeda provides ten keys that balances simplicity and complexity in business, technology, design and life in general. This is the main reason of why in my posters I played with different shapes and typography changing their sizes, color, position, etc. In the beginning, I was using primary colors (red, blue, yellow) but, as I was moving forward, I started to use more variety which you can see along the different versions.
Grids were also another tool we used in this project and I played with column and modular grids as well as diagonal and hierarchical. In the end, I chose the two posters using modular and column grids, and one that uses modular and diagonal. Also, they have more variety of colors in comparison to the other options I had.
