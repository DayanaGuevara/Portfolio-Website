Social Media Campaign

Communication Design I | Spring 2024

For this project, we decided on a brand to work through the semester and mine was Juan Valdez and made different types of campaigns. This one was a social media campaign, and my main goal was to include elements from the previous campaigns and create an interactive activity for the users who were aware of the brand and for the ones who weren't.
My campaign is based on Instagram, and it starts when the user comes across a poster on the Story letting them know about a website where they can create their own characters with assets connected to Juan Valdez and Colombia. After this, they can save them and share them on their own social media accounts spreading the information to reach more and more people. Juan Valdez Instagram account will be sharing posts about the activity as well and will be reposting stories and posts from their ambassadors and employees.
